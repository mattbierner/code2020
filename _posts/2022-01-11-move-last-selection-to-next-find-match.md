Move the current selection to the next instance of the selected text

Windows/Linux: <kbd>ctrl</kbd><kbd>k</kbd> <kbd>ctrl</kbd><kbd>d</kbd>

MacOS: <kbd>cmd</kbd><kbd>k</kbd> <kbd>cmd</kbd><kbd>d</kbd>
